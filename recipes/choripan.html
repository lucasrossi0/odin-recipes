Choripan Recipe

Description

The staple of street cuisine in Argentina is the Choripán, sometimes referred to as "chori" with fondness. It is a grilled chorizo sandwich topped with chimichurri, the nation's signature meat sauce.

Ingredient list:

  • Chorizos
  • Bread
  • Onion
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Prepare the choripán
  2. Add the chorizo and let cook
  3. Make the chimichurri
  4. Place cooked sausage into each roll
