Choripan Recipe

Description

RECIPES This Argentinian Choripán Recipe Wins Summer The country’s spicy sausage sandwiches are fun to make and eat—and can feed a crowd

Ingredient list:

  • Chorizos
  • Bread
  • Onion
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Prepare the choripán
  2. Add the chorizo and let cook
  3. Make the chimichurri
  4. Place cooked sausage into each roll
