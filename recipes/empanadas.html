Empanada Recipe

Description

How to put together empanadas from the start to the end! This is a family dish that has a wonderful meat filling and a light-fried, sugar-coated, fluffy, delicious dough. You will never taste better empanadas than these!

Ingredient list:

  • Beef
  • Chorizo
  • Potato cubes
  • Herb and spices
  • Tomato paste
  • Tomato passata (puree)
  • Chicken stock/broth
  • Garlic and onion
  • Green onion

Steps:

  1. Empanada filling
  2. Empanada dough
  3. Wrapping the empanadas
  4. Baking
